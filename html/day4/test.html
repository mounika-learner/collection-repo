Animal
tiger

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest extant cat species, native to Asia. They are solitary, apex predators, and obligate carnivores. Tigers have a powerful, muscular build and distinctive striped coat. They are known for their role in various cultures and mythologies, particularly in Asian countries.

lion

The lion, often called the "king of the jungle," is a large, powerful cat known for its mane (in males) and social behavior. Lions are apex predators, meaning they are at the top of their food chain, and are typically found in savannas and grasslands of sub-Saharan Africa, with a small population of Asiatic lions in India.
